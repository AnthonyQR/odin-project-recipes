Tocino

Tocino is a sweet a savory Filipino cured meat made from pork belly. This recipe will show one method to cook tocino.

Ingredients

  • One package of tocino
  • Oil
  • Water
  • Optional:Brown Sugar

Tools

  • Stovetop
  • Large pot to fit all of the tocino in
  • Large spoon

Steps

  1. Set the temperature of the stove to high
  2. Put the tocino into the pot
  3. Immediately add oil & water to the pot until it barely covers the tocino
  4. Wait for the water to boil, then set the temperature of the stove to medium-high
  5. While the tocino is softening in the water, break it into pieces with the spoon

    6. Optional: Add some brown sugar to the pot for additional sweetness

  6. Keep boiling the tocino until the water has evaporated, then start stir-frying the tocino
  7. Stir-fry the tocino until you have a few pieces that are slighltly charred / burnt
  8. Remove the tocino from the pot into a serving dish, and enjoy!
