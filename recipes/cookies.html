Cookies

This is a quick & easy recipe to make sweet, delicious & chewy cookies at home!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp. salt
  • 1 tbsp. baking soda
  • 3/4 cup melted or browned unsalted butter
  • 1 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp. vanilla extract
  • 1 tbsp. molasses
  • 1 cup chocolate chips
  • 100 - 150g chocolate bar

Tools

  • Electric mixer
  • Two large bowls
  • Large baking sheet
  • Parchment paper
  • Oven
