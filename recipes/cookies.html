Cookies

This is a quick & easy recipe to make sweet, delicious & chewy cookies at home!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp. salt
  • 1 tbsp. baking soda
  • 3/4 cup melted or browned unsalted butter
  • 1 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp. vanilla extract
  • 1 tbsp. molasses
  • 1 cup chocolate chips
  • 100 - 150g chocolate bar

Tools

  • Electric mixer
  • Two large bowls
  • Large baking sheet
  • Parchment paper
  • Oven

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350F
  2. Whisk the dry ingredients together (flour, salt, baking soda)
  3. With the electric mixer, mix the butter, brown sugar, granulated sugar, vanilla extract, and corn syrup until smooth
  4. Add eggs to the wet mixture and beat until smooth
  5. Add dry ingredients and mix them together until it turns into cookie dough

  7. Scoop cookie dough onto a parchment-lined baking sheet
  8. Bake the cookies in the oven for 15 - 18 minutes
  9. Remove the baking sheet from the oven and let them cool for a bit on the baking sheet, before transfering them to a wire rack to finish cooling
