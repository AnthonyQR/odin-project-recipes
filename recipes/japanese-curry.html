Japanese Curry

Japanese curry is a popular dish that consists of meat, vegetables, and a sauce. This recipe will teach you the general guidlines to make japanese curry with your own meat, vegetables, and curry cubes.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs. of any combination of meats such as:
    • Chicken
    • Beef
    • Pork
    • Seafood
  • 2 lbs. of any combination of fruits & vegetables such as:
    • Potatoes
    • Carrots
    • Onions
    • Bell Peppers
    • Apples
  • Water
  • Oil
  • 200g of curry cubes

Tools

  • Large Pot
  • Large Spoon
  • Stovetop
  • Knife & Cutting Board

Steps

  1. Cut all of the meats & vegetables into approximately 1-inch cubes
  2. Add oil to the pot & stir-fry all of the meat and vegetables on medium heat for around 5 - 10 minutes
  3. Add water to the pot until it barely covers the meat and vegetables
  4. Set the heat of the stove to high
  5. When the water starts boiling, set the heat to medium to start simmering
  6. Keep simmering for around 20 minutes
  7. Remove the pot from the heat & add in the curry cubes
  8. Stir the pot until the curry cubes have dissolved
  9. Put the pot back on the heat at medium-low & keep stirring for 5 minutes
  10. Remove the pot from the heat & serve!
